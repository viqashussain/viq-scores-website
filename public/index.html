viq-scores

Football scores made simple. Introducing viq-scores for all your football match status needs. Available on the Apple App Store.

Over 850 leagues including match scores, line ups, events, match stats etc.

Feature Highlights

Simple UI

Our quick and robust app makes it easy for your to quicky find the score of any football match as well as previously played and future fixture details.

Favourite Leagues

viq-scores makes it easy to set your favourite leagues. This way, you will only be shown match from leagues that you're interested in.

Favourite Teams

viq-scores makes it easy to set your favourite teams. This way, you will only be shown match from teams that you're interested in.

Match Events

View the latest match events as soon as they become available. View the major incidents in the game such as goals (including assists), yellow cards, red cards, own goals etc.

Lineups

View the team line ups as soon as they become available along with their formation in an intuitive lineup. Goals, substitutions, cards and other major events will also be displayed in the lineup so you can stay up to date.

Match Stats

View all the match stats for a match such as shots on target, shots of target, yellow cards, fouls committed, possession etc.
